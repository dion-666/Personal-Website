# Personal Website Plan

## 1) Project Overview
- **Goal**: Personal life journey blog documenting stories, lessons, cycling, and lifestyle.
- **Audience**: Adults aged 35–55 in Indonesia (all genders).
- **Success Criteria**: Grow an engaged readership: newsletter signups, returning visitors, comments, and social shares.
- **Primary CTAs**: Subscribe to newsletter/RSS, read latest story, share an article, contact.
- **Deadline / Milestones**: TBD (set after scoping)

## 2) Brand & Identity
- **Name / Domain**: Name: Dion. Domain: dionjourney.com (selected)
- **Tagline / One‑liner**: Riding through life — stories from the road, sips, and smiles. (Proposed)
- **Tone & Personality**: Humour, joy, cyclist, enthusiast of fine drinks. (Proposed phrasing)
- **Visual Direction**:
  - Colors: Modern and simple; neutrals with a bold accent (Proposed)
  - Typography: Clean, humanist sans for UI; readable serif for long reads (Proposed)
  - Imagery/Style: Minimal layouts, large readable type, editorial feel; real‑life photos (Proposed)
  - Logo/Favicon: Approved to create a simple wordmark “Dion” with a subtle bicycle motif and matching favicon
  - Logo Brief: Wordmark “Dion” in a clean sans; integrate a minimal bicycle outline into the letter "o" or dot of "i"; favicon 1:1 using the bicycle mark or "D" monogram.

## 3) Information Architecture
- **Primary Pages (top‑nav)**:
  - Home (latest posts and featured story)
  - About Dion
  - Blog
  - Contact
- **Secondary Pages**:
  - Blog post template
  - Legal (Privacy, Terms)
 - **Language**: English (confirmed)

## 4) Content Plan
- **About**: Dion — personal background, cycling journey, lifestyle notes, what readers can expect.
- **Projects**: N/A for launch (focus is blog)
- **Blog**: Life journey, cycling, humour, beverages, routines, reflections. Cadence: TBD (Proposed weekly/biweekly).
- **Contact**: Friendly CTA to say hi, share a story, or propose a ride; location/timezone: Indonesia (TBD city)
- **Assets Needed**: Logo, favicon, headshot, lifestyle photos (to be created/curated). Wordmark and favicon approved.

## 5) Features & Functionality
- **Must‑haves**:
  - Responsive design, dark/light mode
  - Fast performance (Core Web Vitals)
  - SEO basics (meta, sitemap, schema)
  - Analytics (privacy‑friendly)
  - Contact form / email routing
- **Nice‑to‑haves**:
  - CMS for blog/content
  - Project filters/search
  - Newsletter
  - Comments (later; skipped at launch)
  - Micro‑interactions/animations

## 6) Tech Stack & Hosting
- **Framework**: Astro + MDX (approved)
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS (approved)
- **CMS (optional)**: Start with MDX files; option to add Contentlayer/Sanity later (approved)
- **Analytics**: Plausible (privacy‑friendly) (approved)
- **Forms**: Formspree (or serverless email via Resend) (approved)
- **Deployment**: Vercel (approved)
- **Performance Targets**: Lighthouse 90+

## 7) SEO & Social
- **Keywords / Topics**: life journey blog, cycling stories, humour, Indonesia lifestyle (refine later)
- **On‑page**: titles, descriptions, headings, alt text
- **Structured Data**: Person, BlogPosting
- **Open Graph / Twitter Cards**: titles, images
- **Sitemap & robots.txt**: yes

## 7.1) Homepage Structure (Draft)
- Hero: Tagline, short intro, subscribe CTA.
- Featured Story: Most recent or curated long‑form post.
- Latest Posts: 6 recent posts with categories (Cycling, Life, Drinks).
- About Teaser: Photo + short bio with link to About.
- Subscribe Section: Buttondown form embed.
- Footer: Links to About, Contact, Privacy, social links.

## 8) Accessibility

# Discovery Questions

## Goals & Audience
1. What is the primary purpose of your site (rank top 3)?
2. Who are your target audiences? Any distinct segments?
3. What outcomes do you want from visitors (CTAs)?

## Brand & Visuals
4. Do you have a domain chosen? If not, any preferences?
5. Describe the tone/personality you want to convey.
6. Any color/typography inspirations or references?
7. Do you have a logo/headshot/photos ready?

## Content
8. What pages do you need at launch?
9. How many projects will you showcase and at what detail level?
10. Will you blog at launch? If yes, how often and about what?
11. What contact methods should be available? (form, email, socials)

## Features & Tech
12. Do you want a CMS or will you edit content in code?
13. Preferred framework/stack or constraints?
14. Any integrations needed (analytics, newsletter, comments)?

## Constraints & Timeline
15. Deadline or event to align with?
16. Time you can invest weekly?
17. Budget for domain, fonts, analytics, etc.?

## Success & Maintenance
18. What does success look like 1 month after launch?
19. What will you maintain vs. automate?
20. Any future features you already envision?
